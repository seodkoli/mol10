Frequently Asked Questions

Is professional carpet cleaning safe for children and pets?

Yes, professional carpet cleaning uses safe and non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for children and pets once the carpets are dry.

Will professional carpet cleaning remove all stains?

While professional carpet cleaning can effectively remove many stains, some stubborn stains may require specialized treatment or may not be fully removable.

Will professional carpet cleaning shrink my carpets?

Professional carpet cleaning, when done correctly, should not cause shrinking. However, improper cleaning techniques or excessive moisture can potentially lead to shrinkage.
